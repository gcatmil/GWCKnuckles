Ugandian Knuckles

Ugandian Knuckles is a meme that became big at the end of 2017 and the beginning of 2018. It had a burst of popularity due to a new Steam game called VR Chat. Someone had created a whacky looking Knuckles skin that was imported into the game and ran around asking people various lines (iconic to him) such as "do you kno de wae."

But, the derpy looking Knuckles VR Chat game to know as Uganda Knuckles did not start here. He was involved within internet culture very early on, his appearance first sighted on YouTube. To find out more, go to our about page.
